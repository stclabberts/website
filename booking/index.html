Join our Abbey Tour today

Embark on a fascinating brewery tour at St. Clabbert's Abbey, where centuries-old brewing traditions come to life. Begin in the historic abbey courtyard, then venture into the stone-walled brewery, rich with the aromas of hops and malt. Discover ancient brewing techniques passed down by generations of monks, and witness the meticulous process of crafting their renowned ales. The tour includes a visit to the aging cellars, where barrels line the walls, followed by a tasting session in the abbey's rustic tavern. Enjoy a variety of handcrafted beers, each brewed with the same dedication and care that define St. Clabbert's Abbey. Book you experience today.
