Crafting Artisanal Beers with Monastic Tradition.

Combining ancient recipes with modern techniques, the brewery produces a range of exceptional ales, from robust stouts to crisp lagers. Each beer is crafted using locally sourced ingredients and pure spring water from the abbey's own wells. Visitors can explore the picturesque brewery, learning about the brewing process and the abbey's rich history. The experience culminates in the tasting room, where guests savor the unique flavors of St. Clabbert's handcrafted brews, each sip reflecting the abbey's heritage and dedication to quality.
